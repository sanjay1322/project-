Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a new service request?
To submit a new request, log in to your account and navigate to the dashboard. Click on the "New Request" button and fill out the required details.
How can I track the status of my request?
You can view the status of all your requests on your dashboard. Each request will have a status indicator, such as "Pending," "In Progress," or "Completed."
